Venus Information

Between Mercury and Earth is the planet called Venus, and is the second terrestrial planet in our solar system. There are some striking similarities between Earth and Venus, such as their similar size and mass, which has earned this planet the nickname of Earth’s “sister” planet. This planet however is not hospitable as the surface temperatures are hot enough to melt lead and have sulfuric acid rainstorms.
