Mars Stuff

The planet that Elon Musk wants to travel to and start a human colony is called Mars. It is the fourth planet from the sun, and is often referred to as the “red planet” because of the large amount of iron-oxide. It is the furthest terrestrial planet in our solar system, and is located outside of the “habitable zone.” This means that water does not exist in a liquid state there, and life being sustained there is unlikely.
